Wigi 4 You

Wigi4You is a Blockchain technology company, headquartered in Japan, with an office in the United States, developing a suite of consumer focused applications, specifically for payment processing.

Wigi 4 You is intended to give retail and e-commerce the ability to accept payment in bitcoin, other digital currencies or local currency (cash). We intend to disrupt the payments industry with the development of our blockchain products and services. We intend to provide retail and e-commerce businesses the ability to accept payment in bitcoin, other digital currencies or local currency. WigiPay will add a small surcharge on every transaction, which we anticipate to be lower than traditional banks and credit cards. Wigi4You believes customers and merchants should have the choice and flexibility to transact in the manner and currency that works best for them in a secure, convenient and cost-effective way. The goal of WigiPay® is to use blockchain technologies to disrupt the outdated, slow and costly payments industry.

Wigi4You products will include WigiPay and WigiPOS. When used together, consumers will be able to pay for products and services using digital currencies, and merchants will be able to accept payment in digital currency with the option of settlement occurring in fiat currency.

WigiPOS will be a simple API program that allows retailers and restaurants a simple way to begin accepting cryptocurrencies. Using a standard tablet for any digital currency transactions, WigiPOS will automatically converts digital currency to local fiat currency and deposits into merchants’ accounts seemlessly.

WigiPay is intended to be the blockchain based technology platform that enables the entire payment processing process to function. With WigiPay, the scaling and transaction speed issues plagued by the digital currency industry, have been solved, and transaction fees are much lower than standard debit and credit card suppliers.
