Wigi 4 You

Wigi4You is a Blockchain technology company, headquartered in Japan, with an office in United States, developing a suite of consumer focussed applications, specifically for payment processing.

Wigi 4 You

is intended to give retail and e-commerce the ability to accept payment in bitcoin, other digital currencies or local currency (cash). We intend to disrupt the payments industry with the development of our blockchain products and services. We provide retail and e-commerce businesses the ability to accept payment in bitcoin, other digital currencies or local currency. WigiPay® adds a small surcharge on every transaction, much lower than traditional banks. Wigi4You believes customers and merchants should have the choice and flexibility to transact in the manner and currency that works best for them in a secure, convenient and cost-effective way. The goal of WigiPay® is to use blockchain technologies to disrupt the outdated, slow and costly payments industry.

Wigi4You products include WigiPay, WigiWallet, and WigiPOS. When used together, consumers are able to pay for products and services using cryptocurrencies, and merchants are able to easily accept payment in cryptocurrency with settlement occurring in fiat currency.

Products

WigiPay

WigiPay is the blockchain based technology platform that enables the entire payment processing process to function. With WigiPay, the scaling and transaction speed issues plagued by the cryptocurrency industry, have been solved, and transaction fees are much lower than standard debit and credit card suppliers.

WigiWallet

Consumers download the WigiWallet app on iPhone or Android, and can use it to pay for various products and services using cryptocurrency, and even to send payments to other users around the globe. WigiWallet is a hosted secure wallet, with a user interface designed to be simple and clean, ensuring that consumers, knowledgable or not, will experience ease of use.

WigiPOS

WigiPOS is a simple API program that allows retailers and restaurants a simple way to begin accepting cryptocurrencies. Using a standard tablet for any cryptocurrency transactions, WigiPOS automatically converts cryptocurrency to local fiat currency and deposits into merchants’ accounts seemlessly.
